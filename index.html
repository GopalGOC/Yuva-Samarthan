ABOUT

We are a group of people with a common goal; to live in a way that we cause minimum harm, and live to do maximum good work. It's our understanding that physical pain is objectively bad. So for us, reducing pain, and nurturing changes that reduce pain is an objective good.

युवा-समर्थन(Youth-Support) is an organization that takes care of stray animal or gives protection, Feeding hungry people wandering in bus stands, government hospitals, and other places, Timely delivery of blood to the needy, Planting of trees, Education to child labour and Trying to curb the evil activities going on in the society. It started when the Corona epidemic was going on in the country, when people were upset, daily wage labourers were trying to go to their home, animals were not getting food due to lockdown, then we felt that now in the society, The time has come to serve in difficult circumstances and then a small group of us got engaged in such tasks.

We started doing good as we believed in doing the right thing. We have observed that doing good is not only good in that sense, but also feels great. And all this was started from Kendua bazar of "Dhanbad" district (also known as coal capital of the country) of Jharkhand state.

OBJECTIVE

1. To work to strengthen and empower the society.

2. To work for the evils prevailing in the society and social upliftment.

3. Working for what the society has never considered to be his own.

4. To provide help to every needy person of the society according to his need.

5. Helping the stray animals on the way.

6. Helping the deranged person living in the society.

7. Provide proper education to orphans.

8. If any person of the society needs blood at any time, provide blood to them on time.
